Cucumber Ice Cream Tacos

Description:

Everyone knows that green is the best color. No one will argue that cucumbers, ice cream, guacamole, and mint are all very refreshing too.

Then it is obvious and no one everyone would agree mixing them is the only option.

Ingredients:

  • 2 each - Plus-size cucumbers
  • 1 tub - Cucumber ice cream
  • 1 bowl - Favorite prepared guacamole
  • Mint leafs

Steps:

  1. Hollow out cucumbers to look like a hard taco shell and put in freezer.
  2. Scoop ice cream to fill "taco shells".
  3. Top with the guacamole.
  4. Garnish with min leafs.

Eat while "shells" are crunchy, and enjoy this summer treat! A classic below the border that has been made for millions of years!
