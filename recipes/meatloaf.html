Martian Marshmallow Meatloaf

Description:

Patriotic meatloaf in remembrance of our victory over the war on Mars in 2025!

Ingredients:

  • 1 - Your favorite cooked meatloaf with large marshmallows inside
  • 1 cup - Mini marshmallows
  • 1 box - Cooked boxed mashed potatoes
  • 1 vial - Martial blood

Steps:

  1. Bring out your cooked meatloaf the way you normally prepare it.
  2. Lay out evenly mini marshmallows on top.
  3. Use a small torch to brown the tops of them, to mimic the rocky mountain of Mars during the war.
  4. Cook mashed potatoes according to box instructions.
  5. Add genuine Martian blood from after the war we won on Mars to the mashed potatoes and stir well.
  6. Display so it resembles the Battle of Olympus Mons on Mars.

Serve immediately and enjoy your meal while we never forget this historic event!
